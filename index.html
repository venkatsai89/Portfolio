Projects

Automotive Review Based Sentiment Analysis

Objective

Our project aims at forecasting the public sentiments of cars which would help the automobile manufacturers and retailers to tweak their manufacturing and sales and hence augment their profit. It even highlights the competitors for a specific brand based on the customer's review. The sentiment scores of a specific model type for a particular car is also displayed based on the customer review.

Data Collection

Data extracted from Kaggel, Twitter, and Youtube Comments

Applicability

The direct application of the project will be in the automobile industries where the automobile specially car manufacturers and retailers can leverage the project to have an understanding of the market based on public reviews and hence tweak their production and upgrades to augment their sales and profit.

Methodology

Text processing like stop words removal, special characters removal, POS tagging is done on the tweets. We will also include the aspect-based mining along with the sentiment analysis to gain the complete usefulness of the data. A time series plot showing variation in sentiment across time is plotted and deep learning technique like Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) is used to forecast the future values.

Scope

The scope of the project is that the project proposed can sustain in the market until there is car sales online, but the only constraint is that the module must be updated when new optimized algorithms emerge or are proposed.
